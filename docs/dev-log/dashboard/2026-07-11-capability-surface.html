Top capability gaps

1

REML is Gaussian-only — allowed for exactly gaussian + biv_gaussian, blanket-rejected for the other 13 families. Even Gaussian REML structured cells cap at diagnostic-only; no supported/inference-ready cell uses REML.

2

No non-Gaussian family reaches "supported." beta/binomial/poisson/nbinom2 reach inference-ready only through unstructured fixed effects; everything else peaks at interval-feasible.

3

Structured effects are sparse and lopsided. 8 families have none; most others expose a single provider on a single dpar. Full richness exists only for gaussian, nbinom2, poisson (mu±sigma) and biv_gaussian (location).

4

Missing-data is narrow. Response masking: 6 families. Predictor mi(): 5 (gaussian broad catalogue; poisson/binomial/nbinom2/beta one binary each). biv_gaussian + 10 others reject both.

5

Non-mu random effects are heavily restricted. sigma-RE rejected everywhere except gaussian (full) and nbinom2 (int-only); shape/inflation dpars are fixed-only nearly everywhere; 5 families allow no random effects at all.
