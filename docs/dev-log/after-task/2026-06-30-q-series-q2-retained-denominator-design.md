# After Task: Q-Series q2 retained-denominator design

## 1. Goal

Turn the reviewed q2 Nibi/Rorqual substitute smokes into a row-level retained-denominator design gate for Tranche 2 without promoting any support cell.

## 2. Implemented

Added `structured-re-q2-retained-denominator-design.tsv`, generated by `tools/summarize-structured-re-q2-retained-denominator-design.R`. The table covers 18 q2 target rows: 12 q2 intercept targets and six q2-plus-q2 within-block targets. Seventeen targets are marked `sr150_pregrid_ready_no_promotion`; the q2-plus-q2 `cor_sigma1_sigma2_intercept` target is held at `profile_repair_required_no_pregrid`.

The Gaussian low-q row-selection sidecar now points the four q2 intercept cells and the q2-plus-q2 cell at this design rather than saying the retained-denominator design is still unwritten. Support cells stay `point_fit/planned/planned`.

## 3a. Decisions and Rejected Alternatives

The design is target-level rather than cell-level because endpoint SD and direct-correlation targets have different interval channels and inheritance risks. q2-plus-q2 is not flattened to a single ready state because the sigma1/sigma2 correlation retained one finite-profile failure in the Nibi substitute smoke.

No DRAC denominator run is launched by this report. The design names the host, artifact, seed, denominator, miss-balance, and stop-rule contract that must be obeyed before a pregrid can become evidence.

## 10. Known Residuals

This is not SR150 or SR475 denominator evidence. It authorizes only the next pregrid shape for the 17 ready targets. The q2-plus-q2 sigma1/sigma2 correlation target remains held until the finite-profile blocker is repaired or explained. No status-table promotion is made.

## 11. Team Learning

For Tranche 2, smoke-reviewed q2 cells should move through a target-level denominator design before compute dispatch. Cell-level wording is too blunt once one q2-plus-q2 target has a retained profile failure while neighbouring targets are pregrid-ready.
