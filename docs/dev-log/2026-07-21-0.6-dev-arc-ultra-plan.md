### The rho12 study — what B2 must pre-register (Fisher)

- **Primary estimand named**, others firewalled: link-scale `delta0,delta1` vs row-specific
  `tanh(eta(w0))` vs residual covariance. Precedent: point bias "earns no coverage credit."
- **Frozen `w0` grid**, and whether `w` is redrawn per replicate — *if `w0` is a realized sample quantile
  the estimand is random and coverage is undefined against a fixed truth.*
- **Pointwise only** (not simultaneous), stated as such.
- **THE TRAP — the coverage denominator is FITS, not fits × rows.** Hits at multiple rows from one fit
  share `delta_hat` and are strongly dependent, so importing the `[0.925,0.975]` exact-binomial gate onto
  row-specific intervals **silently breaks its independence assumption**.
- **Guard policy** pre-declared (`rho12 = 0.999999*tanh(eta)`; the banked artifact shows 2/4
  `fixed_gradient`, 2/4 clamped starts). Per manifest §2a, an exclusion correlated with the estimand
  poisons the clean subset.
- The `delta1 = 0` arm is a **null control**, not evidence for `rho12 ~ x`.
- **Value check:** profile is transformation-equivariant and profile/Wald already agreed to 3 dp at
  n=250. Much of this campaign may re-measure a near-quadratic Wald interval. **The new information lives
  at small n, `rho_xw = 0.5`, and near-boundary `|rho|` — target those or don't spend the compute.**

### B7 promotion standard (Fisher + Rose)

- **Claim-text checklist, reviewers reject on omission**: exact numerator/denominator · MCSE · exact CI ·
  directional miss counts · `profile_finite_rate` · boundary pile-up rate · explicit "Excludes…" negative
  space · conditionality of the guarantee · self-limiting statements (e.g. "no point-bias or Wald claim").
- **Failures are RECORDED in `cells.tsv`, not just promotions** — a promotions-only batch is a
  publication-bias engine across 694 cells. Precedent records negatives (mc-0464 M=8 undercovered).
- **Panel independence**: the D-43 panel for a cell **excludes anyone who touched B1–B6 for that cell**
  (Fisher writes B1/B3, so he cannot sit on that cell's panel).
- **Abort/rollback**: a cell that fails its gate is documented as non-promotion and closed — not retried
  with a loosened gate.
- Mechanics: keep `estimator=ML`; decrement the `point_fit_recovery` count in `test_capability_ledger.py`.
